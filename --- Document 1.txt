# Configuring a Virtual Private Network (VPN) on Windows 11

## Overview
This document provides step-by-step instructions for remote employees to configure the corporate VPN network. Connecting to the VPN ensures all data transmitted between your remote workspace and internal servers is fully encrypted.

## Prerequisites
Before beginning the configuration process, ensure you have:
* A stable home internet connection.
* Your unique 6-digit authentication pin from your company-issued security app.

---

## Step 1: Access Network Settings
1. **Click** the *Start* button on the Windows taskbar.
2. **Select** the *Settings* gear icon from the pinned applications menu.
3. **Click** *Network & Internet* in the left-hand navigation pane.

## Step 2: Input Corporate Server Credentials
1. **Select** the *VPN* option on the right side of the screen.
2. **Click** the *Add VPN* button.
3. **Set** the *VPN Provider* dropdown menu to *Windows (built-in)*.
4. **Type** `DCEA-Remote-Secure` into the *Connection Name* field.
5. **Enter** your assigned server address in the *Server Name or Address* box.
6. **Click** *Save*.

## Troubleshooting Connection Errors
If the connection status displays **"Authentication Failed"**:
* Verify your caps lock key is off.
* Generate a new 6-digit verification pin and re-enter your credentials.
* If the error persists, contact the IT Helpdesk at extension 4102.
