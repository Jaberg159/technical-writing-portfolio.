# Configuring a Virtual Private Network (VPN) on Windows 11

## Overview
Use this guide to set up a VPN on Windows 11. Connecting to the VPN keeps your remote workspace safe and protects all company data while you work. 

## Conditions
Before you start make sure you have:
* A stable home internet connection.
* A 6-digit pin from your company app.

---

## Step 1: Network Settings
1. Click the **Start** button on the windows tab. 
2. Select **Settings** which is the gear icon, from your apps. 
3. Click **Network and Internet** in the left sidebar of the menu.

## Step 2: Inputing VPN
1. Click **VPN** on the right side of the screen. 
2. Click the **Add VPN** button. 
3. Choose **Windows** from the **VPN** that is shown in the menu. 
4. Type `DCEA-Remote-Secure` into the Connection Name box. 
5. Enter your server address into the **Server Name** or **Address box**. 
6. Click **Save**

## Troubleshooting Errors
If you see an **Authentication Failed** error try these steps:
* Check that your **Caps Lock** key is turned off 
* Create a new **6-digit pin** and re-enter your login details. 
* If you are still having trouble, call the IT Helpdesk to get more assistance.
