# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for classroom safety

## Summary
This guide will help with daily safety checklists for morning routines. By following these steps every morning we can meet state licensing rules and guarantee a safe place for all the students. 

---

## 1. Pre-Checks
Complete these checks before you begin your work in the classroom:
* **Observe** The classroom and make sure every toy and other hazards are in a safe place. This will help keep children from slipping and falling when they first walk in. 
* **Check** that the first-aid kit is stocked with supplies. 

## 2. Classroom Environment Safety
Before children enter a room check these items:

* Electrical Outlets: Put plastic safety caps into all electrical outlets.
* Sanitization: Lock all chemicals and cleaning supplies inside of a cabinet.
* Mopping: Any stains, sticky floors, or food on the floor that needs to be cleaned up, should be mopped after the kids walk out of the classroom.

## 3. What to do if a hazard occurs
If any area becomes too hazardous for kids and needs to be fixed, follow these steps:
1. **Write** down the hazard on a sheet of paper. 
2. **Block** off the unsafe area if you need to keep children away from it.
3. **Notify** the center director immediately.
4. **Submit** a request to the repair crew.
