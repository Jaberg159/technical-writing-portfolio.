# Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and ChatGPT are changing how we work with information. AI has made work a lot easier, by generating information we can get work done quicker and easier. However, putting companies personal data into AI isn't all that safe. In order to know how to keep your personal information safe, you'll need to know how different AI tools work. 

---

## 1. How AI Systems Use Your Data
When you type a prompt into a AI system, your data will be used in two different ways:

* Inference: This is where the AI will generate you an immediate answer.
* Retraining: THis is where the AI will save what you type in order for their software to think better.

If an employee copies and pastes details into AI, that information will stay in the AI's system. This will leak data which creates two major risks: 

## 2. The Risks of Data Exposure
Data leaks through AI leave your company vulnerable to:

1. Losing your business secrets: competitors could easily ask AI the same thing you asked it. This will allow competitors to copy your ideas.  
2. Breaking privacy laws: Dropping company data into AI, can violate laws that can lead to fines.  

## 3. Steps to Secure Your Data Privacy
Your team can still use AI as long as they follow these rules: 

1. Use a tech buisness account
Different tech companies offer plans that make sure your data is never used by AI.

2. Don't use data collection services
If you have to use a free version of an AI app, then open your settings, Turn off Data Scraping, History Logging, or Model Training Improvement.

3. Clean up your data before you type
Never put any personal information of the business that you work for like names, or addresses, into AI.
